A Message from Our Pastor

Welcome Home to KBCC

It is my joy and honour to welcome you to the online home of Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre. At KBCC, we believe that every person is created by God with a unique purpose and the potential to be an Answer in their generation.

Our vision is simple yet profound: Raising men and women who will be Answers in the society. Here, we are committed to transforming lives through the Word of God, heartfelt worship, intentional discipleship, and a strong culture of love and service.

Whether you are joining us for the first time, returning after a while, or seeking a place to grow deeper in God, please know that you are welcome, valued, and loved.

Pastor Gbenga Olusanya

Lead Pastor, KBCC

The Name of Jesus (Part 1)

Pastor Gbenga Olusanya KBCC

The name of Jesus is not just a title; it is the greatest spiritual force ever given to humanity. In this teaching, Pastor Gbenga unveils the depth, weight, and glory wrapped inside the name that God Himself exalted above every other name.

You will discover that the name of Jesus carries absolute authority in heaven, on earth, and beneath the earth—meaning no situation, power, or contention is beyond its reach.
