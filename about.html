About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and what we believe

Who We Are

Our Vision

Raising men and women who will be Answers in the society.

At Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC), our vision is to build a people who reflect the life, wisdom, power, and character of Christ—men and women who provide solutions, shine as light, and influence their world for God's glory.

Our Mission

We are committed to gathering the unsaved through intentional evangelism and transforming them by the Word and the Spirit until they become Answers in the society.

We accomplish this through:

  • Passionate soul winning and community outreach
  • Spirit-filled worship and sound teaching of God's Word
  • Discipleship that builds character, purpose, and spiritual maturity
  • Empowering believers to live out their identity in Christ
  • Raising a generation who walk in excellence, love, power, and responsibility

Our Core Values

Righteousness

We uphold a life of purity, integrity, and obedience to God's Word as the foundation for spiritual authority.

Discipleship

Building transformed believers through teaching and mentorship until Christ is fully formed in every member.

Love

We express the love of Christ in our relationships, service, and outreach as the culture of our house.

Faith

We walk by faith, believe God's Word, act on it, and expect supernatural results in every area of life.

Discipline

We cultivate spiritual and personal discipline in prayer, devotion, and excellence.

Evangelism

We passionately pursue souls for Christ, as reaching the lost is at the heart of our assignment.

Responsibility

We take responsibility for God's work, for one another, and for our world.

Excellence

We serve God and humanity with excellence in heart, conduct, and execution.

The Supernatural

We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit, miracles, signs, and wonders as our heritage.

Diligence

We commit ourselves wholeheartedly to the work of the kingdom with lasting impact.

What We Believe

At Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC), our faith is built on the unshakable truth of God's Word and the finished work of Jesus Christ.

The Scriptures

We believe that the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is the final rule for faith, doctrine, and life.

The Trinity

We believe in one God, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, eternally existing in three distinct Persons, co-equal in power and glory.

Jesus Christ

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully God and fully Man. We believe in His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, sacrificial death, resurrection, ascension, and soon return. The Name of Jesus carries all authority in heaven, on earth, and beneath the earth.

Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. There is no other name given under heaven by which men can be saved. We believe in repentance, forgiveness, and new birth through the power of the Holy Spirit.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the person, presence, gifts, and power of the Holy Spirit. He empowers believers to live righteously, walk in the supernatural, and fulfill God's purpose on the earth.

The Believer's Authority

We believe that every believer is called to walk in dominion through the Name of Jesus. We believe in healing, deliverance, miracles, signs, and wonders as part of the believer's inheritance.

The Church

We believe that the Church is the Body of Christ, called to reveal God's glory on the earth. At KBCC, we are committed to raising men and women who will be Answers in the society.

Evangelism and Discipleship

We believe that every believer is called to win souls, make disciples, and expand God's kingdom through intentional outreach, follow-up, training, and community impact.

Christian Living

We believe in living a life of righteousness, holiness, love, faith, diligence, and responsibility. We cultivate spiritual disciplines—prayer, the Word, worship, giving, and service.

Marriage and Family

We believe that marriage is God's covenant institution between a man and a woman. We uphold strong families as a foundation for godly societies.

The Second Coming of Christ

We believe in the literal, visible, and soon return of our Lord Jesus Christ to gather His Church and reign forever.

Eternal Destiny

We believe in the resurrection of the righteous and the unrighteous, eternal life for believers, and eternal separation from God for those who reject Christ.

Our History

May 9, 2013

The Mandate

The mandate of Kingdom of Believers Ministry was delivered by God to the Set Man, Pastor Gbenga Olusanya, during a personal three-day dry fast (May 8-10, 2013). The Lord instructed him to raise a kingdom of believers who live the Kingdom lifestyle and serve as Answers in the society.

November 2014

The Release

After confirmation from his supervising authority at RCCG, Pastor E. A. Odeyemi, Pastor Gbenga and his wife were officially prayed for and released to pursue the divine mandate.

January 29-31, 2015

The Beginning

A three-day crusade was held, marking the foundation of what would become Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre.

February 1, 2015

The Birth

Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC) was officially established in the location God had specifically revealed—Ajeromi-Ifelodun Local Government, Lagos.

2015 - Present

The Journey

KBCC has remained steadfast to its divine mandate, shaping destinies through consistent preaching of the Word of Faith and impactful annual programmes including Believers' Camp Meeting, Kingdom Life Conference, and Easter Growth Retreat.
