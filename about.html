About KBCC

Discover who we are, what we believe, and the vision that drives us.

Our Vision

Raising men and women who will be Answers in the society.

At Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC), our vision is to build a people who reflect the life, wisdom, power, and character of Christ—men and women who provide solutions, shine as light, and influence their world for God's glory.

Our Mission

We are committed to gathering the unsaved through intentional evangelism and transforming them by the Word and the Spirit until they become Answers in the society.

We accomplish this through passionate soul winning, Spirit-filled worship, sound teaching, and empowering believers to live out their identity in Christ.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These ten values shape who we are and how we serve.

1

Righteousness

We uphold a life of purity, integrity, and obedience to God's Word. Righteousness is the foundation for spiritual authority and fruitfulness.

2

Discipleship

We are committed to building transformed believers through teaching, mentorship, and intentional spiritual growth—until Christ is fully formed in every member.

3

Love

We express the love of Christ in our relationships, service, and outreach. Love is the culture of our house and the atmosphere in which lives are healed.

4

Faith

We walk by faith and not by sight. We believe God's Word, act on it, and expect supernatural results in every area of life.

5

Discipline

We cultivate spiritual and personal discipline—prayer, devotion, stewardship, and excellence—because greatness is sustained by consistent habits.

6

Evangelism

We passionately pursue souls for Christ. Reaching the lost and expanding the kingdom is at the heart of our assignment.

7

Responsibility

We take responsibility for God's work, for one another, and for our world. Every member is encouraged to be an Answer and contribute meaningfully.

8

Excellence

We serve God and humanity with excellence in heart, conduct, and execution—because God deserves our best always.

9

The Supernatural

We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit, miracles, signs, and wonders. The supernatural is our heritage and our atmosphere.

10

Diligence

We commit ourselves wholeheartedly to the work of the kingdom, knowing that diligence produces lasting impact and honour.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

At KBCC, our faith is built on the unshakable truth of God's Word and the finished work of Jesus Christ.

We believe that the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is the final rule for faith, doctrine, and life.

We believe in one God, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, eternally existing in three distinct Persons, co-equal in power and glory.

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully God and fully Man. We believe in His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, sacrificial death, resurrection, ascension, and soon return. We believe that the Name of Jesus carries all authority in heaven, on earth, and beneath the earth, and has been given to believers as a rod of divine power.

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. There is no other name given under heaven by which men can be saved. We believe in repentance, forgiveness, and new birth through the power of the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the person, presence, gifts, and power of the Holy Spirit. We believe that He empowers believers to live righteously, walk in the supernatural, and fulfill God's purpose on the earth.

We believe that every believer is called to walk in dominion through the Name of Jesus. We believe in healing, deliverance, miracles, signs, and wonders as part of the believer's inheritance.

We believe that the Church is the Body of Christ, called to reveal God's glory on the earth. At KBCC, we are committed to raising men and women who will be Answers in the society through discipleship, service, love, and excellence.

We believe that every believer is called to win souls, make disciples, and expand God's kingdom. We uphold intentional outreach, follow-up, training, and community impact as core expressions of our mission.

We believe in living a life of righteousness, holiness, love, faith, diligence, and responsibility. We believe in cultivating spiritual disciplines—prayer, the Word, worship, giving, and service.

We believe that marriage is God's covenant institution between a man and a woman. We uphold strong families as a foundation for godly societies and future generations.

We believe in the literal, visible, and soon return of our Lord Jesus Christ to gather His Church and reign forever.

We believe in the resurrection of the righteous and the unrighteous, eternal life for believers, and eternal separation from God for those who reject Christ.

Our Story

A Brief History

The mandate of Kingdom of Believers Ministry was delivered by God to the Set Man, Pastor Gbenga Olusanya, on 9th May 2013 during a personal three-day dry fast held from May 8th to 10th, 2013. In that encounter, the Lord instructed him to raise a kingdom of believers who live the Kingdom lifestyle and who serve as Answers in the society.

At the time, Pastor Gbenga Olusanya was serving as a Traveling Minister with the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG). When God called him into this new assignment, he informed his supervising authority, Pastor E. A. Odeyemi, who insisted he would not release him unless God confirmed it personally. After a season of waiting, Pastor Odeyemi called him and affirmed that indeed, "God has a special assignment for you." In November 2014, he and his wife were officially prayed for and released to pursue the divine mandate.

Following this release, Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC), the church arm of Kingdom of Believers Ministry was birthed. The ministry was established on 1st February 2015, immediately after a three-day crusade held from 29th to 31st January 2015. Remarkably, God had specifically mentioned the exact location where the work should begin, even though Pastor Gbenga had never visited Ajeromi-Ifelodun Local Government before then.

Since inception, KBCC has remained steadfast to its divine mandate: raising men and women who are Answers in the society. Through consistent preaching and teaching of the Word of Faith, and through impactful annual programmes such as the Believers' Camp Meeting, Kingdom Life Conference, and the Easter Growth Retreat, the ministry continues to shape destinies and build believers who shine as solutions in their world.
