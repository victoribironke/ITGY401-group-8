About KBCC

Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC) exists to raise men and women who will be Answers in the society—built by the Word, empowered by the Spirit, and expressed through love and service.

Our Vision

Raising men and women who will be Answers in the society.

We build a people who reflect the life, wisdom, power, and character of Christ—who shine as light, provide solutions, and influence their world for God’s glory.

Our Mission

We are committed to gathering the unsaved through intentional evangelism and transforming them by the Word and the Spirit until they become Answers in the society.

  • Passionate soul winning and community outreach
  • Spirit-filled worship and sound teaching of God’s Word
  • Discipleship that builds character, purpose, and maturity
  • Empowering believers to live out identity in Christ
  • Raising believers who walk in excellence, love, power, and responsibility

Our Core Values

These values shape our culture, our discipleship, and how we serve one another and our city.

Righteousness

Discipleship

Building transformed believers until Christ is formed.

Statement of Faith

What we believe—grounded in the truth of God’s Word and the finished work of Jesus Christ.

1. The Scriptures
We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God—the final rule for faith, doctrine, and life.
2. The Trinity
We believe in one God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—eternally existing in three distinct Persons, co-equal in power and glory.
3. Jesus Christ
We believe Jesus Christ is fully God and fully Man: His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, sacrificial death, resurrection, ascension, and soon return. We believe the Name of Jesus carries all authority and has been given to believers as a rod of divine power.
4. Salvation
We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone; there is no other name under heaven by which men can be saved. We believe in repentance, forgiveness, and new birth by the Holy Spirit.
5. The Holy Spirit
We believe in the person, presence, gifts, and power of the Holy Spirit—who empowers believers to live righteously, walk in the supernatural, and fulfill God’s purpose on earth.
6. The Believer’s Authority
We believe every believer is called to walk in dominion through the Name of Jesus and that healing, deliverance, miracles, signs, and wonders are part of our inheritance.
7. The Church
We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, called to reveal God’s glory on earth. At KBCC, we are committed to raising Answers through discipleship, service, love, and excellence.
8. Evangelism & Discipleship
We believe every believer is called to win souls, make disciples, and expand God’s kingdom through intentional outreach, follow-up, training, and community impact.
9. Christian Living
We believe in living a life of righteousness, holiness, love, faith, diligence, and responsibility—and cultivating spiritual disciplines: prayer, the Word, worship, giving, and service.
10. Marriage & Family
We believe marriage is God’s covenant institution between a man and a woman, and we uphold strong families as a foundation for godly societies and future generations.
11. The Second Coming of Christ
We believe in the literal, visible, and soon return of our Lord Jesus Christ to gather His Church and reign forever.
12. Eternal Destiny
We believe in the resurrection of the righteous and the unrighteous: eternal life for believers and eternal separation from God for those who reject Christ.

A Brief History

The mandate of Kingdom of Believers Ministry was delivered by God to Pastor Gbenga Olusanya on 9th May 2013 during a personal three-day dry fast.

In November 2014, Pastor Gbenga and his wife were officially prayed for and released to pursue the divine mandate. KBCC was birthed and established on 1st February 2015, immediately after a three-day crusade (29th–31st January 2015).

Since inception, KBCC has remained steadfast to its mandate: raising men and women who are Answers in the society through the Word of Faith and impactful annual programmes.
