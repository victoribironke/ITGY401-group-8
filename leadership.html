Leadership

Lead Pastor

Pastor Gbenga Olusanya (PGO)

Lead Pastor, KBCC • President, Kingdom of Believers Ministry

Pastor Gbenga Olusanya is the Lead Pastor of Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC), Lagos, and the visionary founder of The Answer Nation (TAN)—an apostolic movement committed to raising men and women who will be “Answers” in their generation.

He is an author of over sixteen titles, a Certified Life and Relationship Coach, a songwriter, and the author of the Daily Walk Devotional. His ministry is marked by revelatory teaching, practical insights, and the tangible manifestations of the Holy Spirit.

He earned his accounting degree from Babcock University and became a Chartered Accountant while still an undergraduate—an early testament to discipline and excellence.

Co–Lead Pastor

Pastor Yemisi Olusanya (PYO)

Co–Lead Pastor, KBCC • Children Evangelist

Pastor Yemisi Olusanya serves alongside her husband as Co–Lead Pastor of KBCC. She is a passionate minister of the gospel with a dynamic voice to women, families, and especially children.

She is the convener and host of Kids Bible Club with PYO—teaching children biblical truths in simple, relatable, life-transforming ways. She is also the Proprietress of King’s Breed Academy (KBA), a Christ-centered school raising a new generation of leaders.
