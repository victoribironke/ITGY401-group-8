Our Leadership

Meet the leaders God has appointed to shepherd His people

Pastor Gbenga Olusanya (PGO)

Lead Pastor, KBCC | President, Kingdom of Believers Ministry

Pastor Gbenga Olusanya, fondly known as PGO, is the Lead Pastor of Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC), Lagos, and the President of the Kingdom of Believers Ministry (KBM). He is also the visionary founder of The Answer Nation (TAN)—an apostolic movement committed to raising men and women who will be "Answers" in their generation.

PGO is a man graced with remarkable multi-dimensional giftings. He is an author of over sixteen titles, a Certified Life and Relationship Coach, a songwriter, and the author of the well-loved Daily Walk Devotional. Through his books, teachings, and mentoring platforms, he consistently inspires believers to live the kingdom lifestyle and become everything God has ordained them to be.

A teacher of God's wisdom and a voice of clarity in the body of Christ, PGO carries a unique mandate to help people engage divine wisdom to solve real-life problems. His ministry is marked by revelatory teaching, practical insights, and the tangible manifestations of the Holy Spirit. He regularly speaks in conferences, conventions, and seminars across Nigeria and beyond.

Education & Qualifications

  • Accounting degree from Babcock University
  • Chartered Accountant (while still an undergraduate)
  • Principal, Kingdom of Believers Leadership Institute
  • Principal, School of Answers
  • Proprietor, King's Breed Academy

Family

He is joyfully married to Pastor Yemisi Olusanya (PYO), who co-labours with him in ministry. Their union is blessed with four biological children—Praise, Glory, Grace, and Beloved—and numerous spiritual sons and daughters across the world.

Pastor Yemisi Olusanya (PYO)

Co-Lead Pastor, KBCC

Pastor Yemisi Olusanya (PYO) is the Co-Lead Pastor of the Kingdom of Believers Christian Centre (KBCC), Lagos, where she serves alongside her husband, Pastor Gbenga Olusanya (PGO). She is a passionate minister of the gospel and a dynamic voice to women, families, and especially children.

Ministry Focus

A trailblazer in children's ministry, PYO is a Children Evangelist with a strong mandate to shape young lives through the word of God. She is the convener and host of the impactful weekly outreach, Kids Bible Club with PYO, a platform through which she teaches children biblical truths in simple, relatable, and life-transforming ways.

Educational Leadership

She is also the Proprietress of King's Breed Academy (KBA), a vision-driven Christian school committed to raising a new generation of leaders through excellence, character formation, and Christ-centered education.

Known for her warmth, wisdom, and deep love for God's people, PYO continues to inspire and disciple children, teenagers, and families, helping them grow into the fullness of God's purpose for their lives.
